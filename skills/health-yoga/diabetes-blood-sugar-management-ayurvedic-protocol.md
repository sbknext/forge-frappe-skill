# Diabetes & Blood Sugar Management — Ayurvedic Protocol

**Goal slug:** diabetes
**Indian-context relevance:** India is the diabetes capital of the world (101M diabetics, ICMR 2023); Ayurveda's madhumeha chikitsa is the oldest documented diabetes protocol in medical history.

## Primary Ingredients
- **Karela** (Momordica charantia) — charantin + polypeptide-P; insulin-like action; 20ml juice BID
- **Jamun** (Syzygium cumini) — seed powder reduces post-prandial glucose; 1 tsp seed powder BID
- **Methi** (Trigonella foenum-graecum) — soluble fibre (galactomannan); insulin secretagogue; 5g seed soaked overnight + consume AM
- **Haldi** (curcumin) — AMPK activation; insulin sensitisation; 500mg + piperine
- **Amla** — chromium-like activity; HbA1c-lowering (Akhtar 2011 PMID 22214253); 20ml juice AM

## Supporting Ingredients
- **Neem** — insulin receptor sensitisation; blood sugar clearance
- **Giloy** (short-term only) — immunomodulatory in T2DM inflammation
- **Vijaysar** (Pterocarpus marsupium) — beta-cell protection; classic madhumeha specific
- **Gudmar** (Gymnema sylvestre) — destroys taste of sugar; regenerates beta cells; 400mg
- **Tulsi** — insulin secretion support; 2 caps/tea

## Daily Regimen
- **Morning (empty stomach):** Karela-Jamun juice blend 20ml + soaked methi seeds 5g
- **Pre-breakfast:** Nishamalaki churna (Haldi 1 part + Amla 1 part) ½ tsp in warm water
- **With meals:** Haldi-piperine 500mg; Gudmar 400mg (takes 20 min pre-meal for best effect)
- **Bedtime:** Jamun seed powder 1 tsp + Triphala ½ tsp for gut-glucose axis
- **Representative product:** Dia Free Juice blend (karela+jamun+gurmar; varies by brand)

## Timeline & Expectations
- **Week 2–4:** Fasting glucose reduction 10–20 mg/dL; post-prandial spikes reduce
- **Week 8–12:** Measurable HbA1c trend; FBS normalisation in pre-diabetes
- **3 months:** HbA1c review; insulin dose review with physician in T2DM

## Lifestyle Anchors (Charaka non-negotiables)
- Avoid refined carbs (maida, white rice, sugar) — the single biggest driver; herbs reduce, not erase, glycaemic spike
- 45 min post-meal walk — reduces post-prandial glucose as effectively as metformin 500mg in studies
- Manage stress — cortisol raises blood glucose 30–40 mg/dL in T2DM patients

## Cautions for This Goal
- **Giloy HILI warning:** Tinospora cordifolia linked to herb-induced liver injury — Kulkarni 2022 Hepatol Commun, Nagral 2021/2023 J Clin Exp Hepatol. Limit to <3 months; monitor LFTs.
- These herbs LOWER blood glucose — risk of hypoglycaemia with metformin/sulphonylureas; monitor glucose and inform diabetologist
- Never discontinue prescribed antidiabetics without physician guidance; Ayurveda is adjunct therapy
- Karela: hypoglycaemic; avoid in G6PD deficiency; do not use with insulin without monitoring
- Methi: anticoagulant effect at high doses; inform if on warfarin
