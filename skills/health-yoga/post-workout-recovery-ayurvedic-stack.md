# Post-Workout Recovery: Ayurvedic Stack

**Anchor:** Within 30 min of workout completion
**Goal:** Cortisol modulation, muscle recovery, mitochondrial support

## Sequence
1. **Ashwagandha KSM-66 300 mg** — with protein shake or warm milk (not empty stomach)
2. **Warm turmeric milk** — 200 ml milk, ¼ tsp turmeric (Lakadong preferred), pinch black pepper, 1 tsp ghee
3. **Shilajit 250 mg** — for endurance athletes / high-intensity training days only
4. **Rest 20 min** — Shavasana or light walk; avoid cold water bath immediately

## Why (Ayurvedic Rationale)
Exercise creates vata-aggravating movement and pitta-generating heat. Ashwagandha (KSM-66 standardized to ≥5% withanolides) clinically validated for cortisol reduction (Chandrasekhar 2012, *IJAM*). Warm milk + ghee is sneha (oleation) — restores ojas depleted by effort. Shilajit fulvic acid supports ATP production at mitochondrial level; dosing post-workout leverages elevated nutrient uptake window.

## Cautions
- Ashwagandha thyroid interaction: monitor TSH if on levothyroxine (ushna potency, may alter T3/T4)
- Shilajit contraindicated in gout (purine load); reduce/skip on rest days
- Curcumin needs piperine (black pepper) + fat for absorption — 2,000% bioavailability increase (Shoba 1998, *Planta Medica*); fat-free turmeric water is nearly inert
