# Ashwagandha — *Withania somnifera*

**Sanskrit/Regional:** Ashwagandha ("smell of horse"), Asgand Nagori (Unani), Indian Winter Cherry; Amukkuram (Tamil), Penneru (Telugu)
**Active compounds:** Withanolides (withaferin-A, withanolide-D), alkaloids (somniferine, somnine), sitoindosides, withanosides

## Varieties / Grades

| Grade | Withanolides | Source/Method | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Raw powder | <1% | Ground dried root | Baseline; classical churna |
| Nagori (AAA+) | 1.5–2.5% | Nagaur, Rajasthan — drought-stressed | Classical premium; horse-urinous smell authentic |
| KSM-66 | ≥5% | Ixoreal; root-only; milk extraction | 22+ RCTs; most-studied extract |
| Sensoril | ≥10% (higher withaferin-A) | Natreon; root+leaf | 125mg → cortisol ↓24% in 60d RCT |

## Dosage
- **Raw powder:** 3–6g with warm milk + ghee + honey at night
- **KSM-66 extract:** 300–600mg/day (most RCT dose)
- **Sensoril:** 125–250mg/day
- **Max:** 5g/day raw or 600mg/day extract; cycle 8–12 weeks

## Ayurvedic Properties
- **Rasa:** Tikta (bitter), Katu (pungent), Madhura (sweet)
- **Guna:** Snigdha (unctuous), Laghu (light)
- **Virya:** Ushna (hot)
- **Vipaka:** Madhura
- **Dosha:** Vata-Kapha shamak
- **Karma:** Balya (strength), Rasayana (rejuvenative), Vajikarana (aphrodisiac), Medhya (cognitive), Nidrajanana (sleep-inducing)
- **Classical refs:** Charaka Sutrasthana (balya gana); Sushruta Chikitsasthana; Bhavaprakasha (Asgand entry)

## Health Benefits
- **Cortisol/stress:** Multiple meta-analyses confirm ↓cortisol, ↓perceived stress (KSM-66 600mg/d 8wk)
- **Sleep:** PMID 32742886 — KSM-66 600mg/d 8wk improved PSQI sleep quality scores
- **Muscle/strength:** RCTs show lean mass gain, VO₂ max improvement (Wankhede 2015)
- **Testosterone:** Lopresti/Smith RCTs show modest T↑ in stressed/sub-fertile men
- **Female sexual function:** HSDD improvement in RCT (Dongre 2015)
- **Thyroid:** ↑T4 in subclinical hypothyroidism — **caution in hyperthyroid**
- **Anxiety:** Andrade GAD scale improvement; comparable to some anxiolytics
- **Cognition/memory:** Choudhary 2017 RCT — processing speed, memory

## Common Preparations
- **Ksheerpaka:** milk decoction with ghee + jaggery at night (classical)
- **Churna:** mixed warm milk + ghee + honey before sleep
- **Ashwagandharishta:** fermented preparation (liquid)
- **Ashwagandhadi Lehyam:** semi-solid electuary
- **Men's stack:** +Shilajit + Safed Musli + Gokshura

## Sourcing
Rajasthan (Nagaur district) = classical premium; MP largest volume; Punjab/Gujarat/Haryana also cultivated. Manasa, MP = key commercial hub. Genuine Nagori: pale grey (not bleached white), horse-urinous aroma.

## Contraindications
- **Pregnancy:** uterotonic — avoid
- **Hyperthyroidism / on levothyroxine:** ↑T4 interaction — monitor thyroid levels
- **Autoimmune conditions (lupus, RA, Hashimoto's):** immunostimulatory — caution
- **Sedatives/benzodiazepines:** additive CNS depression
- **Antidiabetics:** additive hypoglycemia risk
- **Active peptic ulcer / severe Pitta constitution:** Ushna virya may aggravate

## Adulteration Markers & Certifications
- **Species fraud:** Withania coagulans often substituted
- **Bleached powder:** genuine pale grey, never brilliant white
- **Smell test:** authentic has horse-urinous (ashwa = horse) odor; bland = suspect
- **Certs:** AYUSH Standard/Premium Mark, NPOP organic, withanolide % on COA, single-origin Nagaur labeling
