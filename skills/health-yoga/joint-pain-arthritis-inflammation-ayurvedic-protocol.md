# Joint Pain, Arthritis & Inflammation — Ayurvedic Protocol

**Goal slug:** joints
**Indian-context relevance:** India has 180M+ arthritis patients; Indian vegetarian diet low in omega-3 increases inflammatory load — Ayurveda's amavata (rheumatoid/inflammatory joint) protocol is well-evidenced.

## Primary Ingredients
- **Haldi + Piperine** (curcumin 500–1500mg/d) — COX-2/5-LOX inhibitor; comparable to ibuprofen in mild OA (Kuptniratsaikul 2014); must take with piperine + fat
- **Shallaki/Boswellia** (Boswellia serrata, 65% boswellic acids) — 5-LOX inhibitor; 300mg TID
- **Ashwagandha** — anti-inflammatory + muscle support; 300mg BID

## Supporting Ingredients
- **Guggul** (purified; Yogaraj Guggul formulation) — amavata-specific; 2 tablets TID
- **Giloy** (for RA/amavata) — immunomodulatory; 300mg, max 3 months
- **Shilajit** — cartilage support + anti-inflammatory; 250mg AM

## Daily Regimen
- **Morning:** Ashwagandha 300mg + Shilajit 250mg in warm milk
- **With meals (TID):** Haldi-piperine 500mg + ghee (fat absorption) + Shallaki 300mg
- **Evening:** Yogaraj Guggul 2 tabs (amavata/RA); Ashwagandha 300mg second dose
- **Topical:** Warm sesame oil massage (mahanarayan taila) to affected joints 15 min before bath

## Timeline & Expectations
- **Week 2–4:** Pain intensity reduces (NRS 2–3 points)
- **Week 4–8:** Morning stiffness duration decreases
- **Week 8–12:** Functional improvement; reduced NSAID reliance (with physician)

## Lifestyle Anchors (Charaka non-negotiables)
- Avoid curd (dadhi) at night — classical amavata aggravant
- Anti-inflammatory diet (turmeric, ginger, omega-3 from flax/walnuts) amplifies curcumin effect
- Gentle yoga (not high-impact) maintains synovial fluid circulation

## Cautions for This Goal
- **Giloy HILI warning:** Tinospora cordifolia linked to herb-induced liver injury — Kulkarni 2022 Hepatol Commun, Nagral 2021/2023 J Clin Exp Hepatol. Limit to <3 months; monitor LFTs.
- Curcumin at >1500mg/day: GI discomfort possible; take with food
- Shallaki: mild GI side effects; take with food
- Guggul: anticoagulant — inform physician if on warfarin/aspirin
- Ayurveda adjunct to, not replacement for, DMARDs in confirmed RA
