A structured 30-minute end-of-week review that clears mental debt and sets Monday's direction.

## When to use
Every Friday 5–5:30 PM or Sunday evening. Consistent timing is more important than perfect timing.

## Steps
1. **Collect (5 min)** — Sweep inbox, WhatsApp, notes app, desk surface. Move everything to one capture list.
2. **Complete (5 min)** — Close any task that takes <2 minutes right now.
3. **Review last week (5 min)** — What did I finish? What slipped? Any pattern?
4. **Review next week (10 min)** — What are the 3 must-do outcomes? What appointments exist? Block time for the must-dos now.
5. **Clean up (5 min)** — Archive old notes, clear Downloads folder, close browser tabs.

```markdown
1.

- 

### Open loops to resolve

```

## Tips
- Do it with a hot drink — ritual signals matter to the brain.
- If you miss a week, do a 10-minute abbreviated version rather than skipping entirely.
- The pattern-noticed line is the most valuable — review it monthly for systemic fixes.
