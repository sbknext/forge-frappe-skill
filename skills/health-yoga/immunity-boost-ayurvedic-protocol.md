# Immunity Boost — Ayurvedic Protocol

**Goal slug:** immunity
**Indian-context relevance:** Post-COVID India saw 300% surge in immunity product demand; Ayurveda's vyyadhikshamatva (immune resistance) framework maps closely to modern immunology.

## Primary Ingredients
- **Chyawanprash** (Amla-base, 49 herbs) — classical immunomodulator; 1 tsp AM
- **Tulsi** (Ocimum tenuiflorum, Krishna variety) — antimicrobial + adaptogenic; 2 cups kadha/day
- **Giloy** (Tinospora cordifolia) — immunomodulatory; 300mg extract short-term
- **Haldi** (curcumin 500mg + piperine) — NFkB suppression; anti-infective

## Supporting Ingredients
- **Ashwagandha** — stress-immune axis; 300mg BID
- **Neem** — antimicrobial blood purifier; 250mg cap
- **Amla** — vitamin C (600mg/100g) + tannins; 20ml juice AM

## Daily Regimen
- **Morning:** Chyawanprash 1 tsp + Amla juice 20ml + Tulsi-ginger-pepper kadha (5 tulsi leaves + ½ inch ginger + 2 peppercorns; boil 5 min)
- **Afternoon:** Haldi-piperine 500mg with food
- **Evening:** Giloy tablet 300mg (max 3 months continuous)
- **Bedtime (haldi doodh):** ½ tsp haldi + pinch pepper in 200ml warm milk

## Timeline & Expectations
- **Week 2–4:** Acute infections (cold/flu) less severe and shorter
- **Week 8–12:** Sustained immune resilience; seasonal illness reduction

## Lifestyle Anchors (Charaka non-negotiables)
- Sleep 7–8h — immune memory consolidation occurs in deep sleep; no supplement fixes chronic sleep debt
- Seasonal eating (fresh local produce) — processed food depletes agni (digestive fire)
- Nasal hygiene: daily nasya (2 drops sesame oil in each nostril) blocks pathogen entry at mucosal gate

## Cautions for This Goal
- **Giloy HILI warning:** Tinospora cordifolia linked to herb-induced liver injury (HILI) — Kulkarni 2022 Hepatol Commun, Nagral 2021/2023 J Clin Exp Hepatol. Do NOT exceed 3 months. Monitor LFTs. Avoid in liver disease.
- Chyawanprash contains sugar — use sugar-free variant for diabetes
- Haldi: curcumin needs piperine + fat for absorption; plain haldi in water is largely wasted
- Tulsi: high eugenol in Krishna variety — avoid therapeutic doses in pregnancy
