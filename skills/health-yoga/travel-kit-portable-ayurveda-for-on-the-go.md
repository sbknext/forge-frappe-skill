# Travel Kit: Portable Ayurveda for On-the-Go

**Anchor:** Flights, trains, hotel stays, conference travel — any disruption to routine
**Goal:** Constipation prevention, immunity maintenance, digestion support, sleep under travel stress

## Kit Contents (7 essentials)
1. **Triphala capsules 500 mg** — constipation (travel gut); 1–2 caps at bedtime with warm water (varies by brand)
2. **Tulsi drops 30 ml** — immunity; 5 drops in warm water 2x/day; doesn't need refrigeration (varies by brand)
3. **Haldi capsules 500 mg** — anti-inflammatory (long flights, ankle swelling, jet lag inflammation); 1 cap twice daily with food
4. **Ashwagandha KSM-66 300 mg** — sleep in new timezone, travel stress; 1 cap at night
5. **Hingvashtak churna 25 g sachet** — digestion (airport/hotel food, irregular meals); ¼ tsp in warm water before meals
6. **Neem capsules** — skin (different water quality, hotel skin reactions); 1 cap daily
7. **Copper bottle** — for water storage; Ayurvedic tamra jal (copper-charged water) for digestive support

## Packing Tips
- All capsule forms; no powders in checked luggage (spice confusion at customs)
- Tulsi drops and Hingvashtak double as carry-on liquids — within 100 ml limit if decanted
- Copper bottle passes all airport security

## Cautions
- Triphala laxative: start ½ dose first night to gauge response
- Ashwagandha: may cause drowsiness; take only at bedtime during travel, not before driving
- Hingvashtak: hing-containing — not suitable for pregnancy
- Neem: do not exceed 1 cap/day during short trips; liver caution with long-term high-dose use
