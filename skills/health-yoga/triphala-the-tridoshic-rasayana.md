# Triphala — The Tridoshic Rasayana

**Polyherbal formula:** *Phyllanthus emblica* (Amalaki) + *Terminalia bellirica* (Bibhitaki) + *Terminalia chebula* (Haritaki)
**Sanskrit:** Triphala ("three fruits"), Trivrit, Pathya (Haritaki); individual: Dhatri (Amalaki), Vibhitaka, Abhaya
**Active compounds:** Tannins (chebulinic/chebulagic/emblicanin A&B/gallic), Vitamin C (Amalaki), anthraquinone glycosides, flavonoids

## Classical Ratio — **1:1:1 per Charaka Samhita**
> Many brands deviate: 2:1:1 (Amla-heavy) or 1:1:2 (Haritaki-heavy). Echo Shop discloses exact ratio per SKU.

**Tridoshic balance:** Amalaki pacifies Pitta | Bibhitaki pacifies Kapha | Haritaki pacifies Vata

## Dosage
- **Powder (churna):** ½–1 tsp (2–6g) warm water at night before sleep
- **Tablets:** 2 tablets twice daily (BID)
- **Eye wash:** 1 tsp powder steeped overnight in 200ml water, strained, used to wash eyes AM

## Ayurvedic Properties
- **Rasa:** Five tastes (Pancharasa) — all except Lavana (salty)
- **Virya:** Net balanced — Haritaki+Bibhitaki Ushna + Amalaki Sheeta
- **Vipaka:** Madhura
- **Dosha:** Tridoshic
- **Karma:** Anulomana (mild laxative/bowel regulator), Rasayana, Chakshushya (eye), Medhya (cognitive), Srotoshodhaka (channel cleanser)
- **Classical:** Charaka calls Haritaki "Pathya" (always wholesome); Amalaki "foremost Rasayana"

## Best Paired With
- **Acute constipation:** + castor oil (1 tsp)

- **Classical formulas:** Triphaladi gana, Triphala ghrita, Triphala kwath, Brahma Rasayana

## Contraindications
- **Pregnancy:** Haritaki uterotonic + mild laxative — avoid
- **Active diarrhea/severe depletion:** worsens dehydration/electrolyte loss
- **Children <5:** use lower dose; pediatric Ayurvedic supervision
- **Long-term very high dose:** loose stools, potassium depletion
- **Iron deficiency on iron therapy:** tannins may reduce absorption; separate by 2h

## Adulteration Markers & Certifications
- Bibhitaki/Haritaki shells used as cheap filler (high tannin but less active)
- Look for: organic cert, **1:1:1 ratio disclosed on label**, batch tannin+VitC test, GMP/AYUSH Standard Mark
