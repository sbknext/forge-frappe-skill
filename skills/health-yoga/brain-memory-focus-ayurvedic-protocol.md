# Brain, Memory & Focus — Ayurvedic Protocol

**Goal slug:** brain-memory
**Indian-context relevance:** Competitive academic and professional pressure makes cognitive enhancement India's fastest-growing wellness category; Bacopa monnieri is India's most-studied medhya herb.

## Primary Ingredients
- **Brahmi** (Bacopa monnieri, ≥20% bacosides) — cholinergic enhancement; memory consolidation; 300mg/d (Stough 2001, Roodenrys 2002)
- **Shankhpushpi** (Convolvulus pluricaulis) — medhya rasayana; working memory + focus; 10ml syrup or 500mg
- **Mandukaparni** (Centella asiatica) — NGF (nerve growth factor) stimulation; 300mg

## Supporting Ingredients
- **Ashwagandha** (KSM-66) — stress → cognition mediator; 300mg BID
- **Jatamansi** — executive function + calm focus; 200mg
- **Shilajit** — mitochondrial ATP for neurons; 250mg AM

## Daily Regimen
- **Morning:** Brahmi 300mg + Ashwagandha 300mg + Shilajit 250mg in warm milk
- **Post-breakfast:** Shankhpushpi syrup 10ml in water
- **Afternoon:** Mandukaparni 300mg
- **Bedtime:** Jatamansi 200mg + Brahmi ghrita 1 tsp in warm milk (classical formulation; premium)

## Timeline & Expectations
- **Week 2–4:** Focus duration increases; stress-fog lifts
- **Week 4–8:** Short-term recall improves
- **Week 8–12:** Bacopa's slow-acting memory consolidation peaks (most studies 12-week endpoint)
- Students: start 8 weeks before exams for measurable benefit

## Lifestyle Anchors (Charaka non-negotiables)
- Deep sleep 7–8h — memory consolidation is a sleep-phase process; no herb compensates sleep debt
- Brahmacharya (mental discipline): single-tasking focus over multitasking; social media limits
- Cow's milk with Brahmi — classical anupana enhances nootropic delivery (fat-soluble components)

## Cautions for This Goal
- Brahmi = Bacopa monnieri (NOT Centella asiatica); verify species on packaging — mislabelling common
- Bacopa slow-acting: benefit appears at 8–12 weeks; discontinuing at 4 weeks is premature
- KSM-66 + thyroid medication: monitor TSH
- Shankhpushpi syrup: most commercial formulations contain sugar; diabetics check label
- Mandukaparni (Centella): mild blood-thinning; avoid pre-surgery
