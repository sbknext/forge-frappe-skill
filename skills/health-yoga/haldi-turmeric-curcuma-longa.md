# Haldi (Turmeric) — *Curcuma longa*

**Sanskrit/Regional:** Haridra (Sanskrit), Manjal (Tamil), Pasupu (Telugu), Arishina (Kannada), Haldar (Hindi/Marathi)
**Active compounds:** Curcumin (curcuminoids 2–7%), ar-turmerone, zingiberene, bisdemethoxycurcumin

## Dosage
- **Culinary:** 1–3g/day (½–1 tsp) in food
- **Therapeutic curcumin extract:** 500–1,500mg/day with piperine + fat
- **Raw powder:** max 3g/day; higher doses → GI upset

## Ayurvedic Properties
- **Rasa:** Tikta (bitter), Katu (pungent)
- **Guna:** Ruksha (dry), Laghu (light)
- **Virya:** Ushna (hot)
- **Vipaka:** Katu
- **Dosha:** Kapha-Vata shamak; Pitta-aggravating in excess
- **Karma:** Varnya (complexion), Krimighna (antimicrobial), Kushtaghna (skin diseases), Raktashodhaka (blood purifier)
- **Classical refs:** Charaka Sutrasthana ch.4 Varnya mahagana; Bhavaprakasha Haritakyadi varga

## Health Benefits
- **Anti-inflammatory:** NF-κB inhibition, COX-2 suppression — well-replicated mechanism
- **Liver/metabolic:** NAFLD — ALT/AST reduction (PMC12736498, 2025 meta-analysis)
- **Lipid:** 2025 systematic review shows modest TG/LDL improvement
- **Glycemic:** adjunct insulin sensitivity (multiple small RCTs)
- **Skin:** brightening/Varnya, acne, wound healing topical
- **Immunity:** cold/flu adjunct, antiviral studies
- **Oral health:** antibacterial mouthwash evidence
- **Mood/cognition:** BDNF upregulation preclinical; small RCTs
- **Cancer chemoprevention:** preclinical data strong; clinical translation limited

## Common Preparations
- **Haldi doodh (Golden milk):** ½ tsp haldi + warm milk + black pepper + ghee
- **Ubtan (face pack):** haldi + besan + rose water
- **Kashayam/kadha:** haldi + ginger + tulsi + pepper decoction
- **Kachchi haldi achar:** fresh rhizome pickle (Punjab/UP winter tradition)
- **Classical formulas:** Haridrakhanda (skin), Nishamalaki (diabetes, +amla)

## Sourcing
MH/TG/KA/TN for volume; Lakadong (Meghalaya) and Sangli/Erode for premium GI-certified; buy whole rhizome when possible and grind fresh.

## Best Paired With
- **Piperine (black pepper):** +2,000% curcumin bioavailability (Shoba 1998)
- **Ghee/coconut oil:** fat-soluble curcumin absorption
- **Ginger + Tulsi:** synergistic anti-inflammatory
- **Classical combos:** Haridrakhanda (skin/allergy), Nishamalaki (amla+haldi, diabetes)

## Contraindications
- **Pregnancy:** uterotonic at high doses — culinary fine
- **Gallstones/bile duct obstruction:** stimulates bile production
- **Anticoagulants (warfarin):** mild antiplatelet — consult physician
- **Iron-deficiency anemia:** may reduce iron absorption; separate timing
- **>2g/day:** GI discomfort, loose stools

## Adulteration Markers & Certifications
- **Lead chromate (PbCrO4):** water test — yellow residue sinks, genuine dissolves
- **Metanil yellow dye:** HCl test — turns pink/magenta (synthetic dye)
- **Chalk filler:** HCl effervescence (CO₂ bubbles)
- **Starch:** iodine turns blue
- **Certs to demand:** FSSAI, AGMARK Grade-1, GI tag (Lakadong/Sangli/Erode), NPOP organic, AYUSH Standard/Premium Mark
