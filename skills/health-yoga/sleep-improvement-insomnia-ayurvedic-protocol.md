# Sleep Improvement & Insomnia — Ayurvedic Protocol

**Goal slug:** sleep
**Indian-context relevance:** India's NSSO 2023 data: 40% urban adults report poor sleep; night-shift IT/logistics workers compound vata-pitta imbalance — classic anidra (insomnia) presentation.

## Primary Ingredients
- **Ashwagandha** (KSM-66) — strongest clinical evidence for sleep (PMID 32742886; Langade 2019); 300mg BID; reduces sleep-onset latency
- **Tagar** (Valeriana wallichii) — Indian valerian; sedative; 300mg 1h before bed
- **Jatamansi** (Nardostachys jatamansi) — medhya + nidrajanana (sleep-inducing); 200mg PM

## Supporting Ingredients
- **Brahmi** — racing-mind insomnia; 300mg AM (paradox: daytime Brahmi → better night calm)
- **Shankhpushpi** — calms mental chatter; 10ml syrup at bedtime
- **Sarpagandha** (Rauvolfia serpentina) — medical supervision only; rebound hypertension risk

## Daily Regimen
- **Morning:** KSM-66 300mg + Brahmi 300mg
- **Evening (6–7pm):** KSM-66 second dose 300mg
- **1 hour before bed:** Tagar 300mg + Jatamansi 200mg
- **At bedtime:** Shankhpushpi syrup 10ml in warm milk; Brahmi ghrita 1 tsp in warm milk (classical formulation)

## Timeline & Expectations
- **Week 1–2:** Sleep-onset latency reduces; fewer night-waking episodes
- **Week 4:** Consistent sleep architecture; dream quality improves
- **Week 6–8:** Full sleep cycle normalisation; energy on waking

## Lifestyle Anchors (Charaka non-negotiables)
- Digital sunset 1h before bed — screen light suppresses melatonin; no herb compensates
- Fixed sleep time (same ±30 min daily) — circadian anchor stronger than pharmacology
- Avoid heavy dinner after 8pm; ama from undigested food directly aggravates vata insomnia

## Cautions for This Goal
- Tagar + sedative medications: additive CNS depression — inform physician
- Sarpagandha: NOT OTC; requires Ayurvedic physician prescription; rebound hypertension on stopping
- KSM-66 + thyroid medication: monitor TSH
- Pregnancy: avoid Tagar; Ashwagandha only under supervision
