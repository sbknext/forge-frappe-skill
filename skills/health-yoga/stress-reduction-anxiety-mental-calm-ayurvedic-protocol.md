# Stress Reduction, Anxiety & Mental Calm — Ayurvedic Protocol

**Goal slug:** stress-anxiety
**Indian-context relevance:** Urban Indian professionals face chronic workplace stress; NLMHC 2023 data show 15% anxiety prevalence — adaptogens offer evidence-based support.

## Primary Ingredients
- **Ashwagandha** (KSM-66, ≥5% withanolides) — cortisol reducer; 300–600mg/d; strongest adaptogen evidence
- **Brahmi** (Bacopa monnieri, ≥20% bacosides) — anxiolytic + memory; 300mg AM
- **Jatamansi** (Nardostachys jatamansi) — medhya rasayana; sedative-anxiolytic; 200mg PM

## Supporting Ingredients
- **Tulsi** (Rama/Krishna variety) — cortisol normaliser; 2 cups tea/day
- **Shatavari** — women's stress + hormonal anxiety; 500mg BID
- **Shilajit** — mitochondrial support for adrenal fatigue; 250mg AM

## Daily Regimen
- **Morning:** Ashwagandha KSM-66 300mg + Brahmi 300mg with warm water/milk
- **Midday:** Tulsi tea (2g dried leaf, steep 5 min)
- **Evening:** Shatavari 500mg (women); Tulsi tea second cup
- **Bedtime:** Jatamansi 200mg; Ashwagandha 300mg second dose

## Timeline & Expectations
- **Week 2–4:** Sleep quality improves; subjective calm increases
- **Week 4–6:** Stress resilience improves; reactivity decreases
- **Week 6–8:** Measurable cortisol AM reduction; DASS-21 scores improve

## Lifestyle Anchors (Charaka non-negotiables)
- Dinacharya (consistent wake time) reduces cortisol variability more than any herb
- Abhyanga (warm sesame oil self-massage) 10 min before bath — vata-pacifying
- Reduce stimulants (>2 cups coffee/day counteracts Brahmi's anxiolytic effect)

## Cautions for This Goal
- KSM-66 + thyroid medication: monitor TSH at 4 weeks
- KSM-66 + sedatives/benzodiazepines: additive effect — inform physician
- Ashwagandha contraindicated in autoimmune conditions (lupus, RA, Hashimoto's without supervision)
- Brahmi is Bacopa monnieri — verify; not Centella asiatica
