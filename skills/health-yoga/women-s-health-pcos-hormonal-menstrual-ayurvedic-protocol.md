# Women's Health: PCOS, Hormonal & Menstrual — Ayurvedic Protocol

**Goal slug:** womens-health
**Indian-context relevance:** India has 1 in 5 women with PCOS; artava (menstrual) disorders are artava kshaya/artava vyapat in Ayurveda — a distinct, well-mapped clinical field.

## Primary Ingredients
- **Shatavari** (Asparagus racemosus) — phytoestrogenic adaptogen; rasayana for stri roga; 500mg BID with milk
- **Ashoka** (Saraca asoca) — uterine tonic; bark kashayam during menstrual phase; reduces dysmenorrhoea
- **Lodhra** (Symplocos racemosa) — pituitary-ovarian axis regulator; PCOS-specific; 500mg

## Supporting Ingredients
- **Ashwagandha** (KSM-66) — stress-driven PCOS; 300mg BID (cortisol → androgens pathway)
- **Triphala** — hormonal waste clearance; 1 tsp bedtime
- **Brahmi** — PMS anxiety; 300mg AM

## Daily Regimen
- **Morning:** Shatavari kalpa 1 tsp in warm milk + Ashwagandha 300mg; Brahmi 300mg for PMS phase
- **Afternoon:** Lodhra 500mg with water
- **Menstrual days:** Ashoka kashayam 30ml BID (bark decoction or commercial extract)
- **Bedtime:** Triphala 1 tsp warm water; Shatavari 500mg second dose

## Timeline & Expectations
- **Cycle 1 (Month 1):** Dysmenorrhoea reduces; PMS anxiety eases
- **Cycle 2–3:** Menstrual regularity improves (PCOS cases); flow normalises
- **3 months (3 cycles):** Hormonal panel improvement; AMH, LH:FSH ratio trends improve in PCOS

## Lifestyle Anchors (Charaka non-negotiables)
- Seed cycling (flax/pumpkin follicular; sesame/sunflower luteal) amplifies hormonal rebalancing
- Weight reduction of 5–10% in PCOS restores ovulation — lifestyle + herbs combination critical
- Avoid plastics and xenoestrogen-rich processed food (endocrine disruptors compound artava vyapat)

## Cautions for This Goal
- Shatavari contraindicated in oestrogen-sensitive conditions (fibroids, ER+ breast cancer, endometriosis)
- Lodhra: limited safety data in pregnancy — avoid; discontinue before IVF without physician guidance
- Ashoka bark: avoid in pregnancy (uterine stimulant)
- All three herbs: inform gynaecologist before concurrent use with hormonal therapy (OCP, clomiphene)
