Track birthdays, occasions, and gift ideas year-round so you never panic-buy at the last minute.

## When to use
Set up once in January; maintain year-round. Review monthly.

## Steps
1. List all recurring occasions: birthdays, anniversaries, festivals.
2. For each person, note 3-5 things they've mentioned wanting or needing in conversation.
3. Add calendar reminders at T-30 days and T-7 days for every occasion.
4. When you see a good gift idea (article, product, experience) for someone, log it immediately.
5. When a T-30 reminder fires, order/book. T-7 is the fallback, not the primary.

## Tips
- The ideas bank is the highest-value part. Log ideas in a running note on your phone as soon as you hear them.
- Experience gifts (dinner, workshop, trip day) are harder to find last-minute than products — book early.
- India context: Amazon, Myntra, and Nykaa have standard 7-day delivery; premium handmade gifts from Etsy/local artisans need 2-3 weeks.
