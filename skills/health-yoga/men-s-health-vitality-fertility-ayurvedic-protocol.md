# Men's Health: Vitality & Fertility — Ayurvedic Protocol

**Goal slug:** mens-health
**Indian-context relevance:** India reports declining male fertility (oligospermia rising 20% in decade); Ayurveda's vajikarana (male vitality) shastra is India's oldest sexual medicine tradition.

## Primary Ingredients
- **Ashwagandha** (KSM-66, 600mg/d) — testosterone (Ambiye 2013 PMID 24371462); sperm count + motility; 300mg BID
- **Shilajit** (purified resin) — CoQ10 enhancement + testosterone; sperm concentration improvement (Biswas 2010); 300mg AM
- **Safed Musli** (Chlorophytum borivilianum) — aphrodisiac rasayana; 500mg BID with milk

## Supporting Ingredients
- **Gokshura** (Tribulus terrestris) — LH stimulation → testosterone; 500mg BID
- **Kapikacchu** (Mucuna pruriens, ≥15% L-DOPA) — dopamine precursor; sperm DNA fragmentation reduction; 500mg
- **Amla** — antioxidant protection for sperm; 20ml juice AM
- **Chyawanprash** — ojas builder; 1 tsp AM

## Daily Regimen
- **Morning (empty stomach):** Amla juice 20ml; Shilajit 300mg in warm milk + Chyawanprash 1 tsp
- **Post-breakfast:** Ashwagandha KSM-66 300mg + Gokshura 500mg
- **Post-lunch:** Safed Musli 500mg with milk + Kapikacchu 500mg
- **Evening:** Ashwagandha 300mg second dose

## Timeline & Expectations
- **Week 4:** Energy, mood, and libido improve
- **Week 8:** Testosterone trending up; semen volume increases
- **Week 12:** Sperm count and motility improvement — one spermatogenesis cycle (64 days)
- Fertility: 3–6 months optimal; semen analysis at 3 months baseline

## Lifestyle Anchors (Charaka non-negotiables)
- Avoid laptop on lap, hot baths, tight clothing — testicular heat is primary spermatogenesis killer
- Zinc-rich diet (pumpkin seeds, sesame, legumes) — zinc is sperm's essential cofactor
- Moderate alcohol only; chronic alcohol sharply reduces testosterone production

## Cautions for This Goal
- Shilajit: buy certified brand; heavy-metal contamination risk in unprocessed resin; fulvic acid 15–60%
- Kapikacchu: high L-DOPA; avoid in psychiatric medication (MAOIs, antipsychotics) without physician clearance
- Gokshura: mild diuretic; kidney stone history — consult physician
- All vajikarana herbs: 3-month cycles with 1-month break; assess via semen analysis
