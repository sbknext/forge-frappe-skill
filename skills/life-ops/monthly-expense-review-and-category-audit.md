Review last month's spend in 20 minutes, spot leaks, and set one spending intention for next month.

## When to use
First Sunday of every month. Takes 20 minutes once set up.

## Steps
1. Export or open last month's bank/card statement.
2. Categorise every transaction into 8 buckets (see template).
3. Compare each bucket to your personal baseline (what you expect to spend).
4. Flag any item >₹500/month that you forgot you were paying (subscription creep).
5. Pick ONE spending category to consciously reduce next month. Set a number.
6. Write a one-line intention: "Next month I will spend ≤₹X on [category] by [specific behaviour]."

## Tips
- India context: UPI payments fragment across apps — check PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, and bank statement separately.
- Subscription audit: search statement for recurring round numbers (₹99, ₹199, ₹499, ₹999).
- Don't aim for perfection in categories — 80% accurate is enough for the insight.
