# Shilajit — Himalayan Mineral Pitch

**Sanskrit/Regional:** Shilajatu, Adrija, Girija ("born of rock"), Asphaltum; Silajatu (Bengali), Shilajit (Urdu/Persian)
**Active compounds:** Fulvic acid (15–60%), humic acid, dibenzo-α-pyrones (DBPs), 80+ trace minerals, selenium, iron, zinc, manganese

> Tar-like exudate from rock crevices 3,000–5,000m altitude; Himalaya/Altai/Caucasus. Formed over centuries from compressed plant-microbial matter.

## Varieties / Grades

| Grade | Source | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Raw/unpurified | Any mountain range | **NEVER safe for oral use** — heavy metals, mycotoxins, debris |
| Shuddha (purified) | Water extraction + filtration | Classical standard; safe oral form |
| Himalayan resin | Kashmir/HP/Uttarakhand | Broader trace minerals; classical preference |
| Altai resin | Russia | Sometimes higher fulvic %; milder taste |
| Kashmiri/Gilgit-Baltistan | High altitude premium | Most expensive; Gilgit belt 4,000m+ |

**Fulvic acid reality check:** 15–60% genuine. **>70% claim = red flag** (Rakaposhi Organics analysis; simple extraction limits). Demand batch COA.

## Dosage
- **Resin:** pea-sized amount (250–500mg) dissolved in warm milk or water, once daily morning
- **Cycle:** 6–8 weeks on, 2–4 weeks off
- **Never double dose** — Yogavahi (catalyst) property amplifies all co-administered herbs

## Ayurvedic Properties
- **Rasa:** Tikta, Katu, Kashaya, Lavana — all except Madhura (sweet)
- **Virya:** Ushna (hot)
- **Vipaka:** Katu

- **Karma:** Yogavahi (potency amplifier/catalyst), Rasayana, Vrishya (aphrodisiac), Medhya, Balya, Mutrala (diuretic)

## Contraindications
- **Hyperuricemia/gout:** purine-rich; may raise uric acid
- **Iron overload (hemochromatosis/thalassemia):** enhances iron absorption
- **Pregnancy:** avoid
- **Active infection/acute inflammation:** Ushna virya may aggravate
- **Antihypertensives:** additive blood pressure lowering reported
- **Antidiabetics:** additive hypoglycemia
- **⚠️ NEVER use raw/unpurified Shilajit** — heavy metal toxicity (Pb, As, Hg) + mycotoxins

## Authentication Tests (Mandatory Before Purchase)
1. **Water test:** pure dissolves fully in warm water → golden-brown clear solution, no residue/oil
2. **Flame test:** pure bubbles, turns to ash; no smoke/burning smell (fake has plastic/organic filler)
3. **Alcohol test:** pure does NOT dissolve in alcohol (fake dissolves)
4. **Texture:** pliable at room temp, hardens in refrigerator (reverse = adulterated)
5. **Taste:** intensely bitter, smoky, slightly metallic. **Sweet = fake.**
6. **COA demands:** fulvic acid 15–60%, heavy metals (Pb<10ppm, As<3ppm, Hg<1ppm, Cd<0.3ppm per WHO herbal limits), microbial, mycotoxin tests
